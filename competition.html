RULES

  • Students must attend a school located in Philadelphia.
  • Competitors must be between the ages of 13-18.
  • Students can participate as individuals or in teams of no more than 3 individuals.
  • Project must fit into one of the three following categories: a business or nonprofit idea, research/advocacy, or a community service project.
  • Submitted project must be at least 4 pages (or 4 sections if using a one page design)
  • Project must be coded in HTML and CSS. JS/jQuery and other frameworks may be used, but are not required
  • Extra points will be awarded to responsive sites
  • Site must be coded “by hand” - no use of templates or online site builders (Wix, Weebly, etc.)
  • Final project must be hosted on Github - we'll provide more details closer to submission time
