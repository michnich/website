Camp Description

Have a tech-enthused student between the ages of 10-14 that wants to learn more? The Coded by Kids Summer Coding Camp is a two-week, half day immersion program designed to teach the fundamentals of coding. Through project-based learning, students will gain a foundation in software engineering and web development that will allow them to design and create their own applications. The Summer Session will expose students to the wide variety of opportunities in the field of technology, and get them excited about future careers in tech. Join us for two weeks of making friends, learning to code, and creating awesome projects!


The Coded by Kids Summer Camps will be hosted at the Marian Anderson Rec Center from 9am-12pm, Monday through Friday. No meals will be provided, however there is refrigeration available at the rec center if campers want to bring lunch from home. Before and after care is available for an additional cost. All equipment (computers, laptops, etc) will be provided.
