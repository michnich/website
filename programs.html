Our Programs

Free Weekly Recreation Center Classes

Every Thursday evening (5 - 6pm) and Saturday morning (10 - 11am) Coded by Kids runs classes at the Marian Anderson Recreation Center for children and teenagers between the ages of 5 and 18. These classes are designed to teach students development and digital design through hands on projects.


Recreation Center Tracks

1. Web Development

2. UI/UX Design

3. Data Science

4. Startup Entrepreneurship

School Programs

Coded by Kids works with elementary, middle and high schools to design and implement coding focused in school and after school programs.

Summer Camps

Coded by Kids runs summer camps that are designed to introduce children of all ages to coding concepts.
